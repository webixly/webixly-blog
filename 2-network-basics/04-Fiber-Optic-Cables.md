## Modes of Transmission

### **1. Single-Mode Fiber (SMF)**
- Allows **only one light path** to travel.  
- Offers **very high bandwidth** and minimal signal loss.  
- Used for **long-distance communication** (like undersea cables or WANs).

### **2. Multi-Mode Fiber (MMF)**
- Allows **multiple light paths** (modes) at once.  
- More affordable but suffers from **modal dispersion** (signal distortion).  
- Ideal for **short-range, high-speed networks** (offices, data centers).
